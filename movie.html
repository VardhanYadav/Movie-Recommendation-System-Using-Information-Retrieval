Our AI Technology

CineMatch AI uses cutting-edge machine learning techniques to analyze movie plots, reviews, and metadata. Our system goes beyond simple genre matching to understand thematic elements, emotional tones, and narrative structures at a deep level.

The recommendation engine combines multiple advanced approaches:

  • Content-based filtering using TF-IDF and BERT embeddings
  • Cosine similarity for matching movie vectors
  • Collaborative filtering with user preference data
  • Hybrid recommendation models for best results

This multi-faceted approach ensures you get the most accurate and diverse recommendations possible.
